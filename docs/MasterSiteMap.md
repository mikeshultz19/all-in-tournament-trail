# Master Site Map

This is the approved site structure for the All-In Tournament Trail.

```text
Home

Schedule
  └── Event Information
        └── Registration
              └── Confirmation

Results
  ├── Current Tournament Results
  └── Past Tournament Results
        └── Big Bass stored within each tournament result

AOY Standings

FAQ & Rules
  ├── How It Works
  ├── Official Rules
  │     └── Safety requirements included here
  └── FAQ

Sponsors

Contact
```

## AITT Admin Center

The staff application is separate from public navigation:

```text
Admin Center
  ├── Tournament Information
  ├── News & Announcements
  ├── Tournament Conditions
  └── Tournament Results
```

The dashboard is scoped to a selected Current Tournament. Sponsors are not a
tournament readiness item or management card.

## Global

- Shared header
- Shared footer
- Shared `PageHeader` component
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use
- Social links

## Explicit Exclusions

- No separate archive image gallery
- No separate Big Bass results page
- No separate safety requirements page
- No extra routes without approval
